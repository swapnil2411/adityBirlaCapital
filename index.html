What are the key benefits (Coverages)?

Daily cash allowance

Get a daily cash allowance of Rs.1000 for each day(up to 24 hours) of hospitalization. Avail a double coverage of Rs 2000 each day in the case of the Intensive Care Unit.

No restriction on use

Use your daily cash allowance as an income replacement, for travel expenses on visiting family, on household costs, EMIs and more.

Preserving no claim bonus

Utilise this cash instead of claiming your regular health insurance for small claims.

What is not covered (Excluisons)?

Treatment in case of war-like situations

Treatment arising due to direct or indirect involvement inawar, civil unrest, or similar socio-political hostile situations, bio-hazards etc.

Intentional self-injury

Committing or attempting intentional self-injury or attempted suicide or criminal breach of law.

Weight management programs

Weight management programs or treatment of obesity,including morbid obesity, cosmetic, plastic surgery and all non-allopathic treatments.

Treatments that don't need hospitalization

Treatments that can be done on an outpatient basis, psychiatric or psychological disorders, mental disorders(including mental health treatments), Parkinson & Alzheimer's disease.
